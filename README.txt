
Module 2 Challenge


The purpose of the exercise is to extract specific information from a lengthy list of date. 

The data includes Stock Ticker Symbols with corresponding daily price points over a three (3) year period.

Output of the data in two (2) lists. The first list includes each stock symbol followed by its annual change in price, annual percentage change in price and total volume of transactions for the given year.

A second list is to identify the Stock with the greatest percentage increase for the year, the greatest percentage decrease for the year and the total greatest total volume for the given year.

A copy of the solution VBS Module (Module1.vbs) is included in the GitHub Repository as submission for review and grading.
